Our Team

The Cornerstone staff consists of committed and caring professional men and women who have been trained in how to work with families and individuals in crisis. All staff have appropriate credentials, licenses and certifications. They are a group of clinicians who have experienced being the client as well as the clinician and have passion for helping people become unstuck.
