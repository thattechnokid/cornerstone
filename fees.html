Insurance & Referrals


Cornerstone Family Counseling accepts most major insurance plans. We encourage pre-arranged contact by telephone or email but will handle urgencies and even walk-ins on a case-by-case basis.

Health care providers may refer patients by phone or, if you prefer, by FAX using the following convenient referral form. We will notify you or your office of the initial contact and keep you appraised of your patient's progress.

We appreciate your trust!
